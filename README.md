# Airbnb Market Intelligence Dashboard 📊

1. Project Title

Airbnb Market Intelligence Dashboard


2. Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb market trends, pricing patterns, customer ratings, and review behavior across major cities.
The objective is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support better decision-making in a competitive short-term rental market.


3. Tech Stack

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling


4. Data Source

* Dataset sourced from **Maven Analytics**
* Includes Airbnb listings, pricing, reviews, and host-related data across multiple cities


5. Business Problem

Understanding Airbnb’s rapidly evolving market is challenging due to:
* Large volume of listing data
* Variations in pricing across cities and property types
* Differences in customer satisfaction
* Uneven market contribution by cities
Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify key trends and make strategic decisions.


6. Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive dashboard that:
* Tracks market growth over time
* Analyzes pricing and property distribution
* Evaluates customer ratings and engagement
* Identifies key contributors to the market


7. Key Features & Visuals

🔹 a. Listings Growth Trend (Line Chart)
    * Displays growth of Airbnb listings over time
    * Highlights peak growth and stabilization phase
    Purpose: Understand market evolution and key turning points

🔹 b. Ratings Analysis (City-wise)
    * Compares average ratings across cities
    Purpose: Evaluate customer satisfaction across locations

🔹 c. Market Contribution (Pareto Analysis)
    * Highlights cities contributing the most to total listings
    Purpose: Identify key markets driving platform growth

🔹 d. Review Trends & Distribution
    * Analyzes review patterns over time and user activity
    Purpose: Understand customer engagement behavior

🔹 e. Interactive Features
    * Page navigation (Overview, Ratings, Reviews)
    * Bookmark (View Detailed Data)
    * Filters (City, Room Type, Year)
    Purpose: Enable dynamic and user-friendly exploration


8. Key Insights

* Listings grew rapidly between 2008–2015 and stabilized afterward
* Entire homes dominate the Airbnb market
* Mexico City has the highest average rating
* Superhosts slightly outperform non-superhosts
* Most users contribute only 1–2 reviews
* A few cities account for a large share of listings


9. Business Impact

* Helps identify high-demand markets
* Supports pricing optimization strategies
* Highlights importance of host quality
* Enables better understanding of customer behavior
* Assists in strategic decision-making
